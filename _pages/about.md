After graduating in [Physics of Data](https://pod.dfa.unipd.it/areas-of-specialties/) at the University of Padua, I am currently working as a Junior AI Scientist at [Ipazia SpA](https://ipazia.com/) in Milan.

For my (Master's thesis)[https://thesis.unipd.it/handle/20.500.12608/78382] I studied the emergence of collective intelligence phenomena within LLM-based multi-agent debate systems. More recently, I have focused on investigating and enhancing LLM's capabilities in long-horizon planning. An important achievement in this effort was reaching the first place on the (BrowserGym Leaderboard)[https://huggingface.co/spaces/ServiceNow/browsergym-leaderboard] for the WorkArena-L1 benchmark: a suite of complex web browsing tasks developed by (ServiceNow)[https://www.servicenow.com/]. 

I am passionate about applying machine learning to real-world problems and extracting meaningful insights from data. I enjoy approaching complex problems through the lens of physics and maintain a keen interest in the intersection of AI, physics of complex systems and finance.
